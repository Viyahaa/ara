What is EMEVINEFINANCE?

EmevineFinance is a project built on multi-blockchains, where trade is performed. EmevineFinance provides multiple trading options ranging from decentralized cryptocurrency trading, liquidity mining, staking, NFTs marketplace and Multi-vendor Ecommerce.


ALL-IN-ONE PLATFORM

Finding decentralized exchanges on different blockchains can be overwhelming and tiring.

The first goal of EmevineFinance is to provide various decentralized exchange on over 10 blockchains for individuals on a single platform



Passive income

EmevineFinance seeks to offer an adequate means of earning passive income for its users. EmevineFinance will provide VinePools and Farms, where users can provide liquidity, stake and farm to earn rewards. Relax and let your Vines do the job!!!

NFT Marketplace

We love your Artworks!

NFTS are more than a passing craze.
